GPS and Satellites in the 20th Century

The development and implementation of the Global Positioning System (GPS) in the 20th century revolutionized navigation. By using signals from a network of satellites, a GPS receiver can pinpoint its exact location anywhere on the globe, making navigation more accurate and accessible.

21st Century Developments

Advancements in technology, such as the increasing accuracy of GPS, the development of navigation applications, and the ongoing exploration and mapping of the ocean floors, continue to refine and enhance our understanding of Earth and our ability to navigate it.
